About Soccer

Soccer is the most popular sport in the world, played by millions globally. It is governed by FIFA and played professionally in leagues such as the Premier League, La Liga, and the Bundesliga.

History of Soccer

Soccer dates back to ancient times, with variations played in China and Mesoamerica. Modern soccer was formalized in 1863 in England, leading to the establishment of FIFA in 1904.
